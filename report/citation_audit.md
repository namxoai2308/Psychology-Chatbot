## Claim Boundaries

- The report does not claim the system is clinically validated.
- The report does not claim the system treats or diagnoses depression, anxiety, or stress.
- Benchmark results are framed as prototype engineering evaluation.
- DASS-21 is described as screening/routing support only.
- Yalom factors are used as design inspiration for limited peer-agent behavior, not as full group psychotherapy.
