The system is intended for support-oriented dialogue research. User-facing responses must avoid diagnosis, medication guidance, dependency reinforcement, and crisis substitution. Safety-critical inputs bypass peer dynamics and should direct the user toward immediate real-world support and appropriate local emergency or trusted-person resources.

The benchmark data should be handled as sensitive mental-health-like text even when synthetic. Released examples, transcripts, and audit artifacts should avoid identifying details, institution-specific references, and any claim that the prototype is suitable for deployment. Anonymous submission materials must not include repository URLs, personal acknowledgments, or institutional identifiers.
