This paper argues that mental-health support dialogue should be evaluated beyond empathy. We presented \trace, a Vietnamese student support prototype that treats response generation as an auditable process: the graph tracks route evidence, stage milestones, safety state, intervention history, and peer policy before producing the final response. We also defined VSM, a benchmark protocol for evaluating route fidelity, stage fidelity, safety, peer dynamics, reliability, and latency across \trace, ablations, and prompt baselines.
